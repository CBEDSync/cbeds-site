CBEDSense In development

Make sense of it, together

CBEDSense is the open space where the community discusses topics, shares news and updates, and puts its work forward for others to see and review. It is how we make sense of what exists and what is happening, and what we learn here feeds straight into CBEDSync and CBEDSynergy.

What you can do here

One space, three ways to take part

Sense making is a shared activity. CBEDSense brings the conversation, the signal and the work into one place.

Discuss topics

Open threads on standards, methods and ideas. Ask questions, challenge assumptions and learn from across the ecosystem.

Share news & updates

Post what is happening: new releases, consultations, events and milestones, so the signal reaches the people who need it.

Showcase your work

Add your projects, tools and outputs for the community to see, use and review. Good work becomes visible and reusable.

And it does not stay here

What surfaces, the questions, the signals and the work, flows into the other two roles.
